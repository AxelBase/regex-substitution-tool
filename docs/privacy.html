Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 06, 2025

We collect nothing. We store nothing. Your text never leaves your browser.

This Line-by-Line Regex Substitution Tool is a completely client-side web application. Every character you paste, every pattern you write, and every result you generate is processed exclusively on your device using JavaScript. No data — not even anonymized usage statistics — is ever sent to any server, including ours.

What We Do Not Collect

We intentionally avoid collecting any form of data, including:

  • Input text — your logs, test outputs, or sensitive data
  • Regex patterns or replacement strings
  • IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or device information
  • Cookies, localStorage persistence, or session tracking
  • Analytics, error reporting, or telemetry of any kind

How Your Data Stays Private

All regex substitution happens instantly in your browser’s memory. When you close or refresh the page, everything is gone. There is no backend, no database, no logging, and no third-party services that could access your content.

No Third-Party Scripts

This site uses only Bootstrap from a trusted CDN for styling and layout. That CDN delivers static files only — it does not track visitors or execute tracking code. No analytics providers, no advertising networks, and no social media widgets are loaded.

Security Is Built In

Because your data never travels over the internet, it cannot be intercepted, logged, or exposed in a breach. This makes the tool ideal for working with production logs, PII-containing test data, security reports, or any confidential text.

Children’s Privacy

The tool is safe for all ages and complies with COPPA and GDPR-K by default through its zero-collection architecture.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes will appear on this page. Given our commitment to zero data collection, material changes that affect privacy are extremely unlikely.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository — we’re happy to help.

Your privacy is not a feature — it’s the foundation. Thank you for trusting this tool with your most sensitive text.
